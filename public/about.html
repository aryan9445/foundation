Our Story

Learn about our journey and mission to help stray dogs

How We Started

Founded in 2020, Paws of Hope began with a simple mission: to help stray dogs find their forever homes in India. What started as a small group of passionate volunteers in Hyderabad has grown into a dedicated organization making a real difference in the lives of countless dogs across Telangana.

Our journey began when our founders witnessed the plight of stray dogs in their community. They realized that with proper care, love, and attention, these dogs could become wonderful companions for families in need of a furry friend. Today, we work closely with local communities and veterinary professionals to ensure the best care for our rescued dogs.
