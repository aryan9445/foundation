Volunteer Opportunities

Dog Care

Help with feeding, walking, and providing basic care for our rescued dogs at our Hyderabad shelter.

  • • Morning (7AM-11AM) and evening (4PM-8PM) shifts
  • • Basic training provided
  • • Minimum 4 hours per week
  • • Knowledge of local dog breeds helpful

Adoption Support

Assist with adoption events and help potential adopters find their perfect match. Help with documentation and follow-ups.

  • • Weekend events (10AM-6PM)
  • • Customer service experience helpful
  • • Knowledge of Hindi/English required
  • • Flexible scheduling

Transportation

Help transport dogs to vet appointments, adoption events, and foster homes across Hyderabad and nearby areas.

  • • Own vehicle required
  • • Valid Indian driver's license
  • • Knowledge of Hyderabad roads
  • • Flexible hours
